They provide us a way of seeing what the Athenians thought to be heroic and noble. They also sought to tackle serious topics such as death and the role of certain groups in Greek society. By learning about heroic deeds of the past, we may become inspired to live better lives. By learning about the major societal issues of the past, we may gain perspective and become better equipped to handle conflict in our own lives. If tragedies from the era describe what people should be like, comedies describe how people actually are. While the comedies from the era are ridiculous and over the top, they do provide a way to examine facets of day to day life in Greece which may not be learned from large sweeping histories or books chronicling the numerous wars of the period.
