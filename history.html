Many echo the platitude that history naturally repeats, but this is not the sole reason to study it. We can partially trace the ideological roots of our own society back to Athens and The Age of Pericles.

“To preserve from decay the remembrance of what men have done, and of preventing the great and wonderful actions of the Greeks and the Barbarians from losing their due meed of glory.” -Herodotus.

There is cultural value in studying the actions of those who lived in the past. Shared histories are one of the main things which can bind groups of people together. There are indeed academic arguments which can be made as to why we should study history, but we must not forget that knowledge itself can be fulfilling and can bring people together.
